OUR ACTIVITY

NITIATIVE FOR DEVELOPMENT AND EMPOWERMENT FOR ALL (IDEA-INTERNATIONAL), is a non-profit, nonpolical, nongovernmental and humanitarian organization
established in 2020 and is registered with the government of the republic of Liberia. This organization as the name implies, is to take initiative of women, men, girls
and boys concerning anything that affects their physical, emotional, social, economic and psychological or psychosocial health and wellbeing.

Apostle Ernest Deline is the CEO.
Our vision is to make communities safe and healthy by 2030, to prioritize reproductive health education, to prevent or eradicate all forms of violence
against women and girls by improving gender equality in the homes and communities, to make areas of operations
ideal to live, work, invest and experience a higher standard of living
